Welcome to the Salesforce Galaxy!



This is a unique, interactive resource for information about the topics covered in the 2021 Salesforce Administrator and Platform App Builder certifications.

Feel free to click on the topics in the Home nebula to explore their star systems. Upon arriving, you can review summaries of the topics and click the pages to visit additional sources of information.

The Salesforce Galaxy should not be utilized as a primary study tool for the certification exams. The Salesforce Galaxy is instead intended to be used as a supplemental or review tool alongside other study materials.

Please note that the Salesforce Galaxy was created by a single individual and therefore does not receive regular updates.
